Our Brick Journey

The Legacy of 'Leg Godt'

The name 'LEGO' is an explicit abbreviation of the two Danish words "leg godt", which translates to "play well". It represents our singular guiding principle across every block, theme, and generation.

From our early beginnings in Ole Kirk Kristiansen’s small Danish workshop in 1932 to the advanced interlocking manufacturing arrays of today, our passion centers around inspiring children and collectors to manifest their imagination structurally.
